Pesticides

Herbicides

Used to kill unwanted plants and weeds in the field.

Fungicides

Control fungal diseases and protect crops from spoilage.

Insecticides

Prevent damage by insects and pests that feed on crops.

Biopesticides

Derived from natural materials like animals, plants, and bacteria.

Rodenticides

Used to kill rodents such as rats and mice in crop storage areas.

Nematicides

Control parasitic nematodes that attack plant roots and reduce yield.
